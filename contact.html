Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for TechSpire Talent Hub?

To register, click on the "Register" button and fill out the registration form using your valid cps.edu.np email address. You'll need to verify your email before you can start using the platform.

What types of talents can I submit?

You can submit various types of talents including visual arts, singing performances, dance videos, poetry, blogs, innovative tech projects, and handmade crafts. We welcome all forms of creative expression!

Is there a limit to how many talents I can submit?

There's no limit! We encourage students to share as many of their talents as they'd like. The more you share, the more you can inspire others in the TechSpire community.

How can I edit my profile information?

Log into your account and navigate to the "Profile" page. Click on "Edit Profile" to update your personal information, bio, and talent categories.

Can I delete my submissions?

Currently, you can manage your submissions through your dashboard. For deletion requests, please contact our support team, and we'll assist you with the process.

Who can see my submissions?

Your submissions are visible to all registered TechSpire students. You can control your profile visibility settings in your account preferences.
